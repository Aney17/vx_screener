Total Market Screener

Every stock held across the 11 Vanguard sector ETFs, with trend, momentum, volume, and relative-strength signals. Focus rolls the rest of the row up into one tier — high focus is a fresh, healthy breakout with nothing flagged; watch is still outperforming but with a caution sign (overbought, already extended, an aging breakout, or momentum that's inconsistent across timeframes); skip means there's no active edge right now, or the stock can't be traded cleanly. A stock can show huge relative-strength numbers and still land in "watch" rather than "high focus" — big gains and low risk are different questions. RS breakout flags stocks whose relative strength line just crossed above its own 50-day average and hasn't fallen back below it — the date and age shown is when that started. Ext (ATR) shows how many average true ranges price is stretched above its 50-day SMA — very high readings mean a name is already extended and more prone to a pullback right as you'd be entering.
